About Us

In today's business ecosystem, outsourcing remains a strategic B2B paradigm. Established in 2018, Power Cleaning is a budding cleaning company that provides a suite of cleaning services,ranging from commercial contractual cleaning, upholstery, and facility maintenance. As the brand "Power Cleaning" derives, the trajectory of our company is focused on delivering bespoked services that are customer-centric and time-critical so that our customers can focus on their core business. Therefore, it is Power Cleaning's mission to operate synonymously with our customers' core values. ​ In time to come, Power Cleaning will commit its critical resources and invest in technology to achieve competitive advantage. While internalizing operations, process workflows, contract procurement, service delivery and interoperability of our business functions, we will focus on continuous improvement and strive to emerge as the industry's leading service provider.
